# MobiCova Health

### Digital health infrastructure for Africa — telemedicine, AI health guidance, and insurance, delivered through the partners people already trust.

---

**What it is**

MobiCova Health is a B2B platform that lets insurers, employers, and telcos give their
members instant access to healthcare — a doctor on a video call, AI symptom guidance, and
health-linked insurance — across the **app, WhatsApp, and USSD**, so it works on any phone.
We don't replace the clinic or the insurer; we connect people to care through the licensed
partners they already use, and run the data layer underneath.

**The problem we solve**

Across Nigeria and much of Africa, quality care is hard to reach and harder to pay for.
Insurers and large employers want to offer real health benefits but lack the technology to
deliver them at scale, on the channels their members actually use. Building it in-house is
slow and expensive.

**What members get**

- **Talk to a doctor now** — video or voice consultations with licensed clinicians.
- **AI health assistant** — symptom checks that guide them to the right level of care.
- **Prescriptions, fulfilled** — sent to a pharmacy, with pickup or courier delivery and
  live tracking.
- **Health-linked insurance** — enrol, view cover, and submit claims from their phone.
- **Any phone, any channel** — full app, plus WhatsApp and USSD for feature phones.

**What partners get**

A complete, white-label health platform they can switch on in days, not months: a dashboard
to manage members, enrolments, and claims; analytics on usage and outcomes; their own
branding and login; and a network of clinics and pharmacies that deliver the care.

**How it's built (the differentiator)**

One platform where **every participant is an organisation** — underwriters, companies,
telcos, clinics, and pharmacies — each managed individually, each seeing only their own
data, all overseen from a central admin console. Care flows seamlessly **across**
organisations (a member of one is treated by a doctor in another and gets medicine from a
pharmacy in a third), while every party sees only the slice they need. It's multi-tenant,
secure by design, and ready to scale to new partners and new countries.

**Where it is today**

A working, end-to-end MVP — live, tested, and demo-ready. Members, doctors, pharmacists,
partners, and platform administrators each have a fully functioning experience. Built to
extend: new organisation types, new services, and new markets slot into the same model.
